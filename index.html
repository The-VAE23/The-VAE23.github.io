A Few Accomplishments

Below are some projects that I have been a part off during my education and career.

Team Onyx India

Team Onyx India is the offical aeromodelling team of K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering. As a team they design, build and fly RC aircraft with intention of participating in the SAE Aero Design Competition held in the US every year.
I was part of the team from 2019 to 2022, initially working on the Aerodynamics team to being the captain of the whole team. During my time at the helm I navigated a tenuous period during the pandemic and lead my team to its first participation in the advanced class of the competition, undertaking a challenge never seen before for us. For our efforts we placed 6th globally among 22 teams.

Design Of Rover (CAPSTONE PROJECT)

Designed and manufactured a 6-wheeled robot with the capabilities of semi-autonomous navigation using stereo vision for collision prevention, remote sensing, and data transmission using under the supervision of Dr. Sangita Bansode and Prof. Arati Phadke. The project was an orignal concept floated by my team and myself. For the succesful execution of the project, i.e. design, fabrication and demonstration of working capabilites, we were awarded a grant of INR. 10000, becoming one of only five groups to receive the grant.

Manufacturing of Gas-Turbine Engine

During my career, I have had the opportunity to work on the manufacturing of India's first indigenously manufactured Gas-Turbine Engine. I have been privelaged to be incharge of the process planning and execution of several sheet metal components for the same. In addition I have also worked on several tubings, machined parts and welded assemblies.

Carbon-fibre based drone assembly

I have had the privelage to work on a completely composite based drone. I have been responsible for the assembly of various parts from child to parent assembly stage with the goal of integrating all parts into a single unifrom assembly.
